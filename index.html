ABOUT

The Hunger was founded at Acadia University by Chris Walker as a class project for Venture Creation. What was once a simple idea has transpired into an ambitious pursuit for creating a positive movement and culture of people eager to succeed and help those in need. The Hunger represents your ambition for success in every aspect of your life. As a brand, our mission is to support and share the unique stories of people across Nova Scotia and to inspire those to never settle in the most important parts of life. By providing quality clothing, The Hunger showcases your aspirations and passion, in the comfort of our brand.
